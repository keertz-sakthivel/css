Eau de Parfum (EDP)

With a concentration of 20-30% essential oils, Parfum stands at the pinnacle of the fragrance hierarchy. Also known as Extrait de Parfum or pure perfume, they are the go-to choice for special occasions where a lasting impression is desired.

Eau de Cologne (EDC)

Eau de Parfum, or Eau de Perfume, comes next in the fragrance spectrum. With a concentration of 15-20% essential oils, it balances elegance and affordability. The slightly lower concentration makes it more versatile for everyday use.

Eau Fraiche

Eau de Cologne features an essential concentration between 2 and 4%. It is usually considered a masculine formula with its scent profile, but certain variations are available for women. This perfume type is known for its refreshing qualities.
